<p>
I recently graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in Physics from Sharif University of Technology and am planning to continue my studies with a master’s degree.</p>

<p>
My interests lie in quantum mechanics, electrodynamics, and general relativity. In particular, I’m fascinated by the interplay between gravity and quantum systems, especially topics such as spin-gravity coupling and gravitomagnetism.
</p>

<p>
For me, physics has always been more than a collection of equations. I enjoy following ideas to their foundations, asking why nature works the way it does, and seeing how seemingly unrelated concepts come together into a bigger picture. There is still a lot I don’t know, and that’s exactly what keeps me interested.</p>
  
<p>
This website is where I share my projects, notes, and the things I learn along the way. It’s a snapshot of my journey as I keep exploring theoretical physics and searching for the questions worth spending years on.
